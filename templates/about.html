About CurrHub

Why this project matters and what problem it solves.

The Problem

Many students know what they want to learn, but they don’t know how to structure it into a semester-wise academic plan. Online resources are scattered, and mentors cannot manually create personalized curriculums for everyone.

Our Solution

CurrHub takes inputs like skill, education level, semesters, weekly hours, and industry focus, then generates a structured curriculum with courses, credits, and topic lists.

Why it’s Hackathon-Ready

Verified

Public GitHub repository for heads/mentors to verify code.

Modular

Frontend, backend, and AI can be upgraded independently.

Expandable

Can add PDF export, login, saved plans, and real AI APIs.
