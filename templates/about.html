About CurrHub

Curriculum planning + career guidance in one place.

What problem are we solving?

Students often struggle to convert a skill into a structured semester-wise plan. Also, they don’t know what internships or certifications to choose.

What does CurrHub do?

CurrHub generates:

  • A semester-wise curriculum
  • Course list + credits
  • Topic breakdown
  • Internship role suggestions
  • Certification recommendations
